About

I'm a digital communications professional with 4+ years coordinating programs and translating complex needs into workable solutions. I've been watching AI help my friends navigate burnout, executive dysfunction, and impossible-seeming challenges—not by fixing their brains or guaranteeing outcomes, but by giving them tools that actually fit how they think.

AI can't change your material conditions. It can't guarantee a promotion, pass an exam for you, or eliminate burnout. What it can do is give you tools to manage the difficulty of living in a world built for neurotypical minds—systems that work with your brain instead of against it, validation when you need it, and structure that helps you keep moving without breaking.

I'm not a medical professional. I won't diagnose, treat symptoms, or replace your care team. I will help you leverage AI for organization, communication, and goal-setting—practical skills that complement your existing support.
